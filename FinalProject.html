Why You Should use Universal Design

Ethical According to the World Health Organization, 1 in 6 people experience a “significant disability” Temporary disabilities Financial More people that can access your content can make your content more profitable Logical By starting a website with dynamic universal design elements, one doesn’t have to worry about giving their site messy and limited accessibility add ons later on

What is Universal Design

Normans Design Concepts: Affordance Constraints Feedback Visibility Page Layout: Avoid Clutter and dense displays Consistency Important Items (positioning and levels of importance) Alignment Fluid Layouts (different resolutions) Page/Line Length

How to use Universal Design

Semantic Structure: Regions Headings Lists Tables Forms Responsive Layout Grid Flexbox Tools for evaluating accessibility HTML5 outliner WAVE Silktide Accessibility Checker
